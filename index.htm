About

This HTML5 Game (Pappu Pakia) has been happily made for Tech Talk Live 2018. Just a fun Salt demo!

You are pappu (Paul Williams from UD) in the game, a little character. You need to click the mouse or tap (touch screen) to levitate else pappu will descend. If he hits the top or bottom boundaries that'll end the game.

There will be some obstacles along the way like forks, branches and some enemies who are also known as "pakias". Hitting them will end the game. 3 types of pakias - sad (pull you, Dan Baker from Lancaster Mennonite), happy (push you, Roy Hoover from IU 13), angry (kill you, Dave Light from IU 13). Keep safe distance from them!

There are some collectibles too! Coins (Kelsey Edmond from UD) for points (yellow for 50, pink for 100, red for 500, etc.). Stars (Brian Steigauf from IU 13) for invincibility for a short period. Berries (Mike Li from UD) spawning clones that'll destroy anything that comes in their way!

Collisions are not super strict to make the gameplay a little less difficult.

Hit Enter or Spacebar keys to (re)start the game.
